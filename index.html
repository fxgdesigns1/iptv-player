🪞 Screen Mirroring
Mirror your whole screen to a TV — then go fullscreen in the player.
📱 iPhone / iPad
1. Swipe down → open Control Centre
2. Tap Screen Mirroring
3. Select your Apple TV or AirPlay TV
🤖 Android
1. Swipe down → open Quick Settings
2. Tap Cast or Smart View
3. Select your Chromecast or Smart TV
🍎 Mac
Control Centre → Screen Mirroring → select Apple TV
🪟 Windows
Action Centre → Connect → select your Miracast TV
